About Us

Raksa Oy is a Finnish construction company with over 20 years of experience in residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We pride ourselves on craftsmanship, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Our mission is to create lasting structures that stand the test of time — both aesthetically and structurally.

Products

From high-quality building materials to prefabricated modular units, Raksa Oy offers a range of construction products designed for efficiency and durability. Our products meet strict Finnish and EU quality standards.

Services

We offer a complete range of services including project design, new builds, renovations, structural engineering, and maintenance. Whether it’s a small home extension or a large industrial facility, Raksa Oy ensures every project is completed safely, on time, and within budget.
